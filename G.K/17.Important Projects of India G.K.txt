1.Polavaram Project ( also known as  Indira Sagar Project , is an under construction multi-purpose National project on the Godavari River in the West Godavari District 
   and East Godavari District in  Andhra Pradesh. The project has been accorded national project status by the Union Government of India and will be the last to be 
   accorded the status.Construction began	1941.

2.Kishanganga Project- The kishanganga Hydroelectric plant is a part of a run-of-the-river hydroelectric scheme that is designed
   to divert water from the kishanganga river to a power plant in the Jhelum River basin in Jammu and kasmir. Construction began 
   in 2007.
